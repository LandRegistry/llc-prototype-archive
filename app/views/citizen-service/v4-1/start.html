View local land charges

Use this service to search for legal rights and restrictions (local land charges) on land or property.

An inspection of the Local Land Charges Register is free under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004 (EIR).

The official search, the search of local land charges guaranteed by HM Land Registry, does not include planning and development applications, building controls, or information about road development schemes near to your property. Ask your conveyancer for a CON 29 search to get this information.
