1. Overview

Use this service to find legal rights and restrictions (local land charges) on a property.

Search for free to see local land charges on any property you want to buy. If you decide to make an offer on the property, you’ll pay £15 for an official search, guaranteed by HM Land Registry.

Start now

2. About local land charges

Local land charges (LLC) are legal rights and restrictions on land. For example, a tree preservation order is a local land charge that stops you from cutting down a tree.

3. Why you need to search for local land charges

If you are thinking about buying a house, you need to find out if it has any local land charges on it. This is because local land charges affect what you can do on a property.

This search is usually done by a conveyancer, but HM Land Registry also offers a search of the local land charges register for free. Use the free search to find out more about the property, to help you decide if you want to buy it.

4. Difference between the free and £15 search

tbc

What the free search will show you

  • where the local land charge is
  • description of the local land charge (what it does)
  • date when the charge affects your property
  • contact details for someone from the local authority who can answer questions about this local land charge

What the £15 search will show you

  • official search, guaranteed by HM Land Registry
  • link to documents (you need this if you want to research a charge)
  • local land charge boundary (shows where a charge starts and stops)
