Search for local land charges (LLC) on land or property

Local land charges (LLC) are legal rights and restrictions on land. You need to search for charges on a property if you are buying or selling land. HM Land Registry offers a search of the local land charges register for free, to reduce your conveyancing costs.

HM Land Registry also offers an official search of local land charges. This service costs £15. It is recommended if you proceed to buy or sell, or if you are taking a land dispute to court. You can upgrade to an official search once you have tried the free service.
