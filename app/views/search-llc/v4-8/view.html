How to download these charges

Want to download your search result? Upgrade to an official HM Land Registry search certificate.

Why upgrade?

Download your charges to a secure (tamper proof) PDF. This is digitally signed by HM Land Registry.


With a secure document, you can be confident that:

  • the information is accurate (straight from HM Land Registry’s database)
  • there is no accidental data loss as the document changes hands
  • the data is up-to-date (time stamped)
  • You can also download in CSV and XML (spreadsheets) and JSON (for developers)
