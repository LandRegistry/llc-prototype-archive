Search for local land charges

Before you start check that your search area is on this register.

Use this service to search for restrictions (local land charges) on land or property.

A personal search of the Local Land Charges Register is free under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004 (EIR).

By starting a search you agree to our terms of use.

Start

  • Free search
  • Paid search

It is free to search for local land charges online. You can do this yourself and it takes about 5 minutes.

You can search by:

  • postcode
  • street name
  • address
  • using the map to find a place

What you'll see on a free search result

You'll get a list of local land charges we find in your search area.
See an example of a free search result

At the end of your free search

You can choose to pay £15 to download your list of local land charges. It takes about 2 minutes to pay.
See an example of a £15 search result

What you'll get with a £15 search result

When you pay, you will get a secure (tamper proof) document digitally signed by HM Land Registry. This includes:

  • charge outlines on a map (spatial extent)
  • links to source document, where available
  • free repeat search of the same area (must be done within 6 months)

What's a repeat search?

For 6 months after you've paid, you can search the same area again, for free. To do this:

  • open a free account and repeat the search yourself
  • contact HM Land Registry (we can do this for you)

Open a free account if you want to save your paid search result online

If you do lots of searches like this one, you can open an account. With an account, you can keep all your lists of charges in one place (your search history) so you won't lose your search results. Sign in or register now.
