Search for local land charges

Before you start, check your search area is on this register.

Use this service to search for restrictions (local land charges) on land or property.

A personal search of the Local Land Charges Register is free under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004 (EIR).

The official search, the search of local land charges guaranteed by HM Land Registry, does not include:

  • * planning and development applications
  • * building controls
  • * information about road development schemes near to your property

If you need this information, ask your local authority for a CON29 enquiry.

By starting a search you agree to our terms and conditions.
