How to use the map

How to draw your search area

Click the polygon button

  • Choose a place and click to start drawing
  • Keep clicking around the edge of your search area
  • To complete your shape join the ends up or double click
How to draw your search area (video)

How to edit your search area

Click the edit button

Your search area will:

  • turn yellow (outline)
  • fade and blur (background)
  • Click and drag the yellow point to edit
  • Click the edit feature button to finish drawing
How to edit your search area (video)

How to delete your search area

Click the remove all button

By clicking the remove button it deletes everything you have drawn on the map.

How to delete your search area (video)

How to copy a building from the map

Click the copy basemap feature button

You can copy from the map when you are zoomed in to a location (seeing a place close-up). Make sure you can see building outlines on the map.

  • Clieck on the building you want to copy
  • The building will be highlighted
How to copy a shape from the map (video)

How to undo the last thing you drew

Click the undo last action button

Clicking this button will delete the last point you drew.

How to undo the last thing you drew (video)

How to draw a shape with snap-to (snap-to)

Click the polygon button

Click the snap-to button

You can only use snap-to when you are zoomed into a location (seeing a place close-up).

  • Hover your cursor near the line or point you want to snap to
  • The cursor will jump to that point automatically
