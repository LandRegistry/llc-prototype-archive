How to use the map

How to draw your search area

Click the polygon button

  • Choose a place to start drawing and click on that point
  • Keep clicking around the edge of your search area
  • Double-click to finish drawing
How to draw your search area (video)

How to edit your search area

Click the edit button

When it is ready to edit your search area will:

  • turn yellow (outline)
  • fade and blur (background)
  • Click on your search area
  • Drag the search area until you have finished editing
  • Double-click to finish editing
How to edit your search area (video)

How to delete your search area

Click the delete all button

By clicking the delete button it removes all search areas on the map

How to delete your search area (video)

How to copy a shape from the map

Click the copy button

Use the map buttons to copy a shape from the map.

You can copy from the map when you are zoomed in to a location (seeing a place close-up). Make sure you can see property outlines on the map if you want to copy.

  • Clieck on the property you want to copy
  • The property will be highlighted

How to finish drawing a shape automatically (snap-to)

Click the snap-to button

Use the map buttons to finish drawing your shape automatically on the map. This will automatically jump from the point you are drawing to another nearest point on the map.

  • Hover your cursor near the line
  • The shape will jump to that point or draw along that line automatically
