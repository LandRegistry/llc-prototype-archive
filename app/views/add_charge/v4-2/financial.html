This is a general financial charge if:

  • the local authority charges a fee for doing work on a property
  • the authority does not know the cost of the work (yet)
  • you don’t have information about a fee, for example ‘amount originally secured and rate of interest, if payable: £600 + Int. @ 20%’

This is a specific financial charge if:

  • the local authority charges a fee for doing work on a property
  • the authority knows the cost of the work
  • • you have information about a fee, for example ‘amount originally secured and rate of interest, if payable: £600 + Int. @ 20%’
